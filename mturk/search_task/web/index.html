Welcome to our study!

This is a psychology experiment in which you will be presented images for a very short interval, after which you will need to classify what you saw by choosing between two options. You will be able to select your option by using the 'F' and 'J' keys on your keyboard. After you make your selection, you will be able to press the 'Space' key to continue to the next image. Some images will be presented for longer intervals than others. The task will take about 4 minutes to complete.

Before the task begins, there will be a brief training session. This training session will be identical to the actual experiment in every way, except that your responses will not be recorded. The purpose of the training session is to give you a better understanding of the task.
