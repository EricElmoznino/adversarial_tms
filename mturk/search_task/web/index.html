Welcome to our study!

This is a psychology experiment in which you will be shown several images and will need to select one target as quickly as possible. As soon as you see the target image in the group, you will need to press the 'Space' bar to stop the timer. All images will then disappear, and you will have to use your mouse to select the location where you saw the target image. Be as quick as you can for pressing the 'Space' bar. You can take your time for selecting the location of the target image with your mouse. You will get feedback about whether or not your answer was correct. Scroll down on this page to see a pictorial description of the experiment.

Before the actual experiment begins, there will be a brief training session. This training session will be identical to the actual experiment in every way, except that you will be shown the target image and feedback for a longer duration. The purpose of the training session is to give you a better understanding of the task.

There will be 6 training sessions and 54 sessions in the actual experiment. The entire task will take about 4 minutes to complete.
