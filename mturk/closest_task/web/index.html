Welcome to our study!

This is a psychology experiment in which you will be shown several target images and will need to select one option among many that you think looks most like the target. Most of the time, the options will look like abstract patterns and will not resemble anything real. Regardless, try your best to select the option that you subjectively feel most resembles the target.

Before the actual experiment begins, there will be a brief training session. This training session will be identical to the actual experiment in every way, except that your responses will not be recorded. The purpose of the training session is to give you a better understanding of the task.

There will be 6 training trials and 54 trials in the actual experiment. The entire task will take about 4 minutes to complete.
